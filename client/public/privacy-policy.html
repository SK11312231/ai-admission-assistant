1 Introduction

Welcome to InquiAI ("we", "our", or "us"), an AI-powered WhatsApp admission assistant built for coaching institutes and educational businesses in India. InquiAI is operated by its founders and is accessible at inquiai.in.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform, and your rights regarding that data.

By using InquiAI, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

2 Information We Collect

Account & Business Information

  • Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Coaching institute name, website URL, and business description
  • WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) ID and phone number details
  • Billing and subscription information (processed via third-party payment providers)

Lead & Conversation Data

  • Names, phone numbers, and messages of prospective students who contact your institute via WhatsApp
  • Conversation history between your WhatsApp bot and leads
  • Lead status, tags, follow-up notes, and custom fields you create

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, features used, and interactions within the InquiAI dashboard
  • Device type, browser, IP address, and approximate location
  • Error logs and performance diagnostics

AI Training Data (Optional)

  • WhatsApp chat exports you voluntarily upload to train your AI assistant
  • Custom Q&A pairs and knowledge base content you provide

3 How We Use Your Data

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the InquiAI platform
  • To power the AI assistant that responds to your leads on WhatsApp
  • To personalise your dashboard and analytics
  • To send service-related emails (onboarding, billing, feature updates)
  • To detect spam, abuse, and fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To provide customer support

We do not sell your data or your leads' data to third parties. We do not use your leads' data to train any general-purpose AI models.

4 WhatsApp & Meta Integration

InquiAI integrates with the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By connecting your WhatsApp Business Account, you acknowledge that:

  • Message data flows through Meta's infrastructure as per WhatsApp's Privacy Policy
  • You are responsible for obtaining consent from leads before messaging them on WhatsApp
  • You must comply with WhatsApp Commerce Policy and Meta's terms
  • InquiAI acts as a data processor; you (the institute) remain the data controller for your leads' data

5 Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Infrastructure (Railway, PostgreSQL), AI APIs (Groq/Llama), email services — all bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Meta/WhatsApp: As required to operate the WhatsApp Business API
  • Legal Compliance: When required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with prior notice to users

6 Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days after account deletion
  • Lead and conversation data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Upon account deletion request, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics may be retained indefinitely

7 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Hashed and salted password storage
  • Environment-based secret management (no hardcoded credentials)
  • Role-based access control within the platform
  • Regular security reviews

However, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required.

8 Your Rights

As a user of InquiAI, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your leads and conversation data in CSV format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@inquiai.in. We will respond within 15 business days.

9 Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We may use analytics tools to understand usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

10 Children's Privacy

InquiAI is a B2B platform intended for use by coaching institutes and educational businesses. It is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top and notify registered users via email if changes are material. Your continued use of InquiAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
